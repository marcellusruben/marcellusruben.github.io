Lear CC-SC Project

CC-SC is an important procedure in the design phase that the design and project engineer at Lear need to do before deploying a proposal regarding the final design of vehicle seat structure.

The Fundamentals of Reinforcement LEarning

Reinforcement learning is probably one of the most relatable scientific approaches that resemble the way humans learn about things. Every day, we, the learner, learn by interacting with our environment to know what to do in certain situations, to know about the consequences of our actions, and so on. When we were babies, we didn’t know that touching a hot kettle would hurt our hand. However, as we learned about how the environment responded to our action, i.e touching the hot kettle hurts our hand, then we learned not to touch a hot kettle. This illustrates the fundamental theory of reinforcement learning.
